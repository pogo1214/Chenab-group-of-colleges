Admissions

Welcome to Chenab Group of Colleges (CGC). We are excited to help you start your academic journey with us. Here you will find all the information you need about our admission process, requirements, and important deadlines.

Admission Procedures

  1. Explore Programs: Browse our diverse range of programs to find the one that best fits your interests and career goals.
  2. Check Requirements: Review the specific admission requirements for your chosen program, including academic qualifications and any additional criteria.
  3. Prepare Documents: Gather all necessary documents, such as transcripts, certificates, and identification.
  4. Apply Online: Complete the online application form and upload the required documents. Alternatively, you can download the application form and submit it in person.
  5. Submit Application: Ensure all sections of the application are filled accurately and submit it before the deadline.
  6. Application Review: Our admissions committee will review your application and notify you of the decision.
  7. Acceptance and Enrollment: If accepted, follow the instructions to confirm your enrollment and begin your studies at CGC.

Requirements

General Requirements:

  • Completed application form
  • Official transcripts from previous educational institutions
  • Copy of national identification card or B-Form
  • Passport-sized photographs (recent, blue background)
  • Any additional requirements specific to the program (e.g., entrance exam scores, portfolio, interview)

For detailed requirements, please refer to the specific program page on our website.
