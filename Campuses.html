Gujrat Campus

The Gujrat Campus of Chenab Group of Colleges (CGC) stands as our flagship institution, offering a comprehensive array of academic programs and state-of-the-art facilities. Renowned for its contemporary infrastructure, distinguished faculty, and dynamic student body, the campus cultivates an optimal learning atmosphere with modern classrooms, advanced laboratories, extensive libraries, and exceptional sports amenities.

Campus Life

Life at CGC Gujrat is vibrant and enriching, with a wide array of clubs, societies, and events happening throughout the academic year. Students are encouraged to engage in diverse extracurricular activities, from sports and cultural festivals to academic competitions. Our campus promotes a strong sense of community, offering abundant opportunities for both personal development and professional advancement.
